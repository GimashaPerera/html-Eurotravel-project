Netherlands

Canals, cycling culture, golden-age art—and spring tulip seas from Amsterdam to Keukenhof.

Reserve a package Talk to an expert

Highlights

  • Amsterdam: Canal belt, Rijksmuseum & Van Gogh Museum.
  • Keukenhof & Tulip Fields: Seasonal bloom (mid-Mar to mid-May).
  • Zaanse Schans & Volendam: Windmills, cheese, clogs & old harbors.
  • Utrecht & Delft: Cathedral towers, blue-and-white ceramics.
  • Rotterdam: Striking modern architecture & foodie markets.

Sample 5-day Itinerary

  1. Day 1 – Amsterdam: Canal cruise & Jordaan neighborhoods.
  2. Day 2 – Art & History: Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh; Anne Frank House.
  3. Day 3 – Keukenhof/Tulips: Bike among fields; Leiden old town.
  4. Day 4 – Windmills & Villages: Zaanse Schans + Volendam seafood.
  5. Day 5 – Delft & Rotterdam: Pottery workshop & Markthal lunch; departure.

Good to Know

  • Best time: Apr–Sep (spring blooms or sunny cycling), Dec for festive lights.
  • Transport: Compact, rail-friendly; bikes everywhere—ask for easy routes.
  • Passes: Museumkaart / I amsterdam for savings on sights.
