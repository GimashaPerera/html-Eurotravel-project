Highlights



  • Lisbon: Tram rides, old town charm, and vibrant nightlife.
  • Porto: River cruises, port wine cellars, and medieval bridges.
  • Algarve: Sunny beaches, sea caves, and coastal hikes.
  • Sintra: Fairytale palaces and lush gardens.
  • Madeira & Azores: Volcanic islands, hiking, and breathtaking views.

Sample 6-day Itinerary



  1. Day 1 - Lisbon Arrival: Explore Alfama & Baixa; enjoy fado music.
  2. Day 2 - Sintra: Visit Pena Palace and Quinta da Regaleira.
  3. Day 3 - Porto: Douro River cruise & wine tasting.
  4. Day 4 - Coimbra: Visit historic university & old town.
  5. Day 5 - Algarve: Relax on beaches & explore caves.
  6. Day 6 - Departure: Free time in Lisbon or Porto.

Good to Know



  • Best time: Apr-Jun & Sep-Oct for pleasant weather.
  • Transport: Trains, buses, and scenic coastal drives.
  • Cuisine: Seafood, pastel de nata, and world-famous wines.

Why Visit Portugal?

Portugal is a land of golden beaches, charming cities, historic castles, and breathtaking coastlines. From Lisbon's lively streets and Porto's riverside wine cellars to the dramatic cliffs of Algarve and the magical palaces of Sintra, every corner offers something unique. A blend of tradition and modern charm makes Portugal one of Europe's most loved destinations.
