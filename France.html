Highlights

  • Paris: Louvre, Eiffel Tower, Montmartre cafés & Seine cruises.
  • Provence & Côte d’Azur: Lavender fields, hill towns, Nice & Cannes.
  • Bordeaux & Burgundy: Legendary vineyards and cellar tastings.
  • Loire Valley: Storybook châteaux and cycling paths.
  • Normandy & Brittany: Mont-Saint-Michel, WWII sites, rugged coasts.

Sample 6-Day Itinerary

  1. Day 1 – Paris Arrival: Seine cruise & Eiffel Tower by night.
  2. Day 2 – Art & History: Louvre fast-track + Left Bank food tour.
  3. Day 3 – Versailles: Palace & gardens; pâtisserie class.
  4. Day 4 – Loire Valley: Chambord & Chenonceau with wine tasting.
  5. Day 5 – Provence: Avignon, Gordes, Roussillon ochre trail.
  6. Day 6 – Côte d’Azur: Nice old town, Eze perfume atelier; departure.

Good to Know

  • Best Time: Apr–Jun & Sep–Oct (milder weather, fewer crowds).
  • Transport: High-speed TGV links Paris with major regions.
  • Cuisine: Bistros, boulangeries & Michelin experiences—ask for our food-first routes.
