About Euro Travel

Euro Travel is a specialist travel company dedicated to crafting memorable, worry-free holidays across Europe. With a passionate team of travel consultants and strong local partnerships, we design personalised itineraries — whether it's a romantic getaway in Venice, a food and wine tour in Tuscany, or an activity-packed trip to the Alps.

We combine years of industry experience with local knowledge to ensure each trip runs smoothly. Our consultants handle everything from flights and transfers to accommodations, guided tours and special requests so you can enjoy a seamless travel experience.

Our Certification & Standards

Euro Travel operates under licensed travel agency regulations and partners with accredited suppliers. We follow robust quality checks and customer service standards to ensure reliable, safe travel for all our clients.

  • IATA accredited partners
  • Preferred supplier relationships across Europe
  • Secure booking & payments
  • Fully compliant with EU travel regulations
