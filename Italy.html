Discover the Magic of Italy

Italy is a feast for the senses—where history, art, and cuisine come alive. Wander through the romantic streets of Venice, admire Rome’s ancient landmarks, and soak in the rolling hills of Tuscany. From authentic pasta and gelato to world-class wines and vibrant piazzas, every moment is a celebration of Italian culture. Italy isn’t just a destination—it’s an experience that lingers in your heart long after you leave.

Highlights



  • Rome & Vatican City: Ancient ruins, iconic landmarks, and rich history.
  • Florence & Tuscany: Renaissance art, charming streets, and rolling vineyards.
  • Venice: Canals, gondola rides, and unique architecture.
  • Milan & Lake Como: Fashion capital, lakeside beauty, and historic cathedrals.
  • Amalfi Coast & Cinque Terre: Stunning coastal views, colorful villages, and seaside adventures.




Sample 6-day Itinerary



  1. Day 1 - Rome Arrival: Explore Colosseum, Roman Forum, and enjoy a local dinner.
  2. Day 2 - Vatican City & Rome: St. Peter’s Basilica, Sistine Chapel, and Piazza Navona.
  3. Day 3 - Florence: Visit Uffizi Gallery, Duomo, and stroll along Ponte Vecchio.
  4. Day 4 - Tuscany: Wine tasting, countryside villages, and scenic drives.
  5. Day 5 - Venice: Gondola ride, St. Mark’s Basilica, and explore canals.
  6. Day 6 - Milan & Lake Como: Visit Milan Cathedral, stroll Lake Como, and departure.



Good to Know



  • Best time: Apr-Jun & Sep-Oct for pleasant sightseeing, Dec-Feb for winter sports in the Alps.
  • Transport: Extensive trains, high-speed rail, and scenic coastal drives.
  • Cuisine: Pasta, pizza, gelato, and regional specialties.
